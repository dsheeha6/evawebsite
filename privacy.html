Privacy Policy

We may collect personal information that you provide directly, such as your name, email address, organization, and project details when you submit forms or contact us.

We use your information to respond to inquiries, provide services, communicate about projects, and improve the website experience.

3. Cookies and Analytics

This site may use cookies or similar technologies for basic functionality and aggregated analytics. See the Cookie Policy for more information.

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with service providers as necessary to operate the site or deliver services, subject to confidentiality obligations.

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete your personal information. To exercise these rights, contact us at hello@evasheehan.com.

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is completely secure.

8. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact hello@evasheehan.com.
