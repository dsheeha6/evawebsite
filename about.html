About Eva Sheehan

Strategic designer and creative director helping organizations communicate with clarity, purpose, and impact.

"Good design is obvious. Great design is transparent."
— Joe Sparano

I'm Eva Sheehan, a strategic designer and creative director with over 8 years of experience helping organizations communicate with clarity, purpose, and impact. My approach combines editorial expertise with strategic thinking to create meaningful visual experiences that connect with audiences and drive results.

My work spans editorial design, brand identity, digital publishing, and marketing communications. I believe that great design starts with understanding—understanding your audience, your goals, and the unique story you want to tell. This foundation allows me to create solutions that are not only visually compelling but strategically sound.

I've had the privilege of working with clients ranging from startups to established corporations, from independent authors to major publishing houses. Each project brings new challenges and opportunities to push creative boundaries while delivering measurable results.
