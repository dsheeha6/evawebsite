Cookie Policy

What cookies we use and how you can control them.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device to store data that can be recalled by a web server in the domain that placed the cookie.

2. How We Use Cookies

We may use essential cookies for site functionality and preference cookies to remember your settings. We may also use anonymized analytics to improve the site.

3. Managing Cookies

You can control and delete cookies through your browser settings. Disabling certain cookies may impact site functionality.

4. Third-Party Cookies

Some pages may embed third-party content (e.g., fonts, scripts) that can set their own cookies subject to their policies.

5. Updates

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page.

6. Contact

Questions about this policy? Email hello@evasheehan.com.
